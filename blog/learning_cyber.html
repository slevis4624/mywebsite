Learning Cybersecurity

March 13, 2026 • Charelston, SC

To keep knowledge coming and to improve my technical skills, I’ve been busy learning cybersecurity fundamentals using TryHackMe.

This platform allows you to take courses which are mostly lab-based which helps you learn on the go rather than listening and reading lecture style content all the time. They have challenges and practice modes to help you solidify your learning fundamentals. They have their own virtual machines and containers so that you can create and break systems for learning and not worry about your own system.

As of this post, I am on track to finishing my pre-security fundamentals.

So far I have learned about...

  • Network Fundamentals
  • Web Fundamentals
  • Computer Fundamentals
  • Operating System Fundamentals
  • Software Fundamentals

Foundations and fundamentals are very important and should be practices more often than even every day to maintain proficiency and mastery of any subject you are learning. Including mathematics!
